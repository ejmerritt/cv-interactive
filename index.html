Profile

I am a graduate student at Goethe University interested in various aspects of language acquisition and bilingualism. My dissertation research deals with English-Mandarin bilingual children's acquisition of recursive structures in their two languages. Previously, I have also done some work on codemixing, as well as early L2 learners' productions of English consonant clusters.
